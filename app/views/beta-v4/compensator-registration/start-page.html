Register or settle a compensation recovery claim online

Use this service to register either a motor liability or public liability claim with the Compensation Recovery Unit (CRU).

This service is for insurance companies and other compensators, not individuals.

Compensators must register a compensation claim within 14 days of receiving it.

By registering claims online, youâ€™ll be able to download your CRU and NHS charges certificates in PDF format.

When not to use this service

Do not use this service if:

  • your claim involves employer liability or clinical negligence
  • the injured person attended an NHS hospital, or used an NHS ambulance
  • the injured person does not have a National Insurance number

You must complete a CRU1 form instead.

Before you start

You'll need the injured person's National Insurance number.

Registering online takes around 5 minutes.
