User Agreement

The User Agreement sets out our terms and conditions for the use of the digital service. It outlines both the rights and responsibilities of compensators and DWP.

The organisation administrator should review it and be familiar with its contents.

It also includes information around data sharing arrangements between both parties.

It contains:

  • principles and guidelines of sharing data with DWP
  • data items and how they will be shared
  • information about personal data breaches and dispute resolution

! Warning The User Agreement must be accepted by a person with authority to make personal data process decisions on behalf of your organisation.
