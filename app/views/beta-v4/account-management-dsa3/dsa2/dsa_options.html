Summary of changes

To register claims on this service, your organisation must:

  • create an account
  • accept the User Agreement (either as part of the account creation stage or at another time)

You should check that you are the right person to create an account on behalf of your organisation.

After creating an account and accepting the User Agreement, you can:

  • ask employees to create individual accounts
  • register and manage claims
  • take over existing claims from other organisations
