We have updated our User Agreement

The User Agreement sets out our terms and conditions to use this digital service. It outlines the rights and responsibilities of compensators and DWP.

It contains:

  • principles and guidelines of sharing data with DWP
  • data items and how they will be shared
  • information about personal data breaches and dispute resolution

What you need to do

! Warning You need to accept the updated User Agreement by 15 October 2024 to maintain access to your account.

After this date, you will not be able to register new claims and manage existing ones until you have accepted the updated User Agreement.

The User Agreement must be accepted by a person with authority to make personal data process decisions on behalf of your organisation.

  • If you have any questions about the User Agreement, send an email to: cru.ebt@dwp.gov.uk.
