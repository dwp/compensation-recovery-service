Create an individual account

You’ll need:

  • access to the email address you want to use for the account
  • to create a memorable password

When you have created an account and your organisation has accepted the User Agreement, you’ll be able to register and manage a new claim online if:

  • it is for motor or public liability
  • the injured person is represented and can provide an email address for their representative
  • the injured person did not receive NHS (HS in Northern Ireland) hospital treatment as a result of the incident

You will not be able to manage claims previously registered by post or third party software through this service.
