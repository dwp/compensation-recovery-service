You have created an account to manage your compensation recovery claims. We have sent you a confirmation email.

What happens next

After your organisation has accepted the User Agreement, you can:

  • register and settle claims
  • get certificates for claims registered on this service that do not have recoverable benefits or NHS charges (HS charges in Northern Ireland)
  • notify Compensation Recovery Unit that you are taking over a claim from another organisation

Requesting certificates

You can only request a certificate using this service for claims registered online.

If you use a third party software to register claims, you can use the CRU
e-service to request a certificate.

For claims registered by post, send an email to cru.highvolumeareacertificates@dwp.gov.uk to request a certificate. You must include the CRU reference (3 letters followed by 3 numbers) in your email.
