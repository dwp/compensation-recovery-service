Register compensation recovery claims

This service is for:

  • insurance companies
  • other compensators
  • third party administrators

Use it to register claims with the Compensation Recovery Service (CRS).

Compensators must register a compensation claim within 14 days of receiving it.

Youâ€™ll be able to download CRS and NHS charges certificates in PDF format.

You can sign in if you already have an account for this service.

Create an account for your organisation to register claims

Your organisation needs to nominate an employee to create an account and use this service. You should check that you are the right person to do this before you go through this process.

This takes around 5 minutes. You will only need to do this once.

After creating an organisation account, you can:

  • invite employees to create individual accounts
  • register and manage claims
  • take over existing claims from other organisations

Other ways to register

You can also register claims by post.

It takes longer to receive a certificate by post than if you register a claim online.
