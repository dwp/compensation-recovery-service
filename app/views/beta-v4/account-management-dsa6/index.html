Register compensation recovery claims

This service is for organisations that register compensation claims with the Compensation Recovery Unit (CRU).

Register a claim online if:

  • It is for motor or public liability
  • The injured person is represented and can provide an email address for their representative
  • The injured person did not receive NHS hospital treatment because of the incident

Other ways to register

If a claim does not meet all of the above criteria, you will need to register it by post using a CRU1 form.

What can you do on this service?

After creating an administrator account and accepting the User Agreement, you can:

  • register and manage claims
  • get Certificates of Recoverable Benefits and Certificates of NHS Charges (HS Charges for Northern Ireland) with nil charges only for claims registered on the service
  • take over existing claims from other organisations
  • manage user accounts for people from your organisation who need to register compensation claims
