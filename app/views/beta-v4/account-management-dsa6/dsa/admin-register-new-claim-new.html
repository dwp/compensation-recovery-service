Register a new claim

Register claims online to download your Certificates of Recoverable Benefits and Certificates of NHS Charges.

Register a claim online if:

  • it is for motor or public liability
  • the injured person is represented and can provide an email address for their representative
  • the injured person did not receive NHS hospital treatment as a result of the incident

Before you start

You’ll need:

  • injured person’s details
  • injury details
  • names and addresses of any hospitals attended because of the incident
  • injured person’s representative details

Registering a claim takes around 5 minutes.
