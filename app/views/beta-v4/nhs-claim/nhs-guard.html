NHS or HS treatment details Did the injured person receive NHS or HS hospital treatment?

HS refers to Health and Social Care treatment specific to Northern Ireland.

This includes:

  • treatment at any NHS or HS hospital
  • treatment at any NHS or HS urgent care centre
  • a trip in an NHS or HS ambulance

This does not include:

  • treatment at an NHS or HS GP surgery
  • treatment at a private hospital
  • a trip in a private ambulance
