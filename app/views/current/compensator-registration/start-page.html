Register a compensation recovery claim online

This service is for insurance companies and other compensators, not individuals.

Use this service to register a road traffic accident compensation claim with the Compensation Recovery Unit (CRU).

Compensators who receive a claim for compensation must register the claim within 14 days.

Registering the claim does not mean that you have accepted liability for the accident or injury.

By registering a claim online, we can send your CRU certificate and NHS charges certificate to you by email.

When not to use this service

Do not use this service if:

  • your claim involves public liability, employer liability or clinical negligence
  • the injured person received any NHS hospital treatment for their injuries, including an ambulance trip
  • the injured person does not have a National Insurance Number

You must complete a CRU1 form instead.

Before you start

You can only use this service to register road traffic accident claims (also known as motor liability).

You'll need the injured person's National Insurance number to use this service.

Registering online takes around 5 minutes.

You can also register by post.

It takes longer to receive a certificate when you register by post than online.
